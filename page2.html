Road to the Final

Overview

The FIFA Club World Cup 2025 will feature 32 teams for the first time, held in the USA. Fans can expect exciting matches, famous stadiums, and the best clubs from every continent.
The tournament will take place from June 8 to June 28, 2025, with matches in cities like New York, Los Angeles, and Miami.
