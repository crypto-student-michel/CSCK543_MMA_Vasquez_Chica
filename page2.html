Road to the Final

Final: Chelsea vs PSG


The “Road to the Final” of the 2025 FIFA Club World Cup has been a showcase of elite football, involving 32 teams across 62 matches. From the group stages to the knockout rounds, only two clubs remain: Chelsea FC and Paris Saint‑Germain. They will meet on July 13th, 2025, in the final held at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey.


Chelsea earned their place in the final through an unexpected yet remarkable campaign. After qualifying via victory in the UEFA Conference League, they advanced from Group D—finishing behind Flamengo—then beat Benfica (4–1), Palmeiras (2–1), and Fluminense (2–0) in the knockouts. Under manager Enzo Maresca, Chelsea have become a cohesive side known for dynamic midfield play and clutch finishing, standing just 90 minutes away from their second ever Club World Cup title.


Paris Saint‑Germain, on the other hand, have maintained dominance throughout the tournament. Winners of Ligue 1, the Coupe de France, and the Champions League (5–0 over Inter Milan), PSG topped Group B and then shut out Bayern Munich (2–0) and Real Madrid (4–0) in the knockout rounds. With manager Luis Enrique at the helm, PSG have exhibited tactical mastery, defensive rigidity, and offensive firepower—backed by standout performances from players like Ousmane Dembélé, who scored 35 goals during the season.


The final promises to be a thrilling encounter, with Chelsea looking to upset the odds against a formidable PSG side. Fans worldwide are eagerly anticipating this clash of titans, which will not only crown the champion of the 2025 FIFA Club World Cup but also etch another chapter in football history.
